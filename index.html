About Me

I'm a frontend developer with a strong eye for design and detail, turning creative ideas into interactive, high-performing web experiences. With 1+ years of freelance experience, I specialize in building responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SASS, Bootstrap, and Tailwind, and have expertise in WordPress, Elementor, and other CMS platforms. I’m also skilled in design tools like Figma and Photoshop to create visually appealing and user-friendly designs.
